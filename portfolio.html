ABOUT_PRADEEP

I'm Pradeep Modak, the creator of this portfolio. I earned a Bachelor's degree in Engineering and computational mechanics which I will be completing in 2027. My hope is that this course will give me knowledge of Design and gave me a career direction. Till 2030, I set this goal to set up my own independent business. Fast forward to 2023, i am trying to upskill myself in the field of programming and designing.

I have always had a passion for drawing since I was a child. In 2020, during a lockdown, painting became a new hobby of mine because I discovered more of my artistic talent. Though Design is my expertise due to studying, Art is a priority of mine, which I incorporate into my business by selling my artwork and taking on client commissions. Art is very special to me, and I don't limit myself when it comes to it. In terms of the mediums I use, I like to mix them up - I am open to Acrylic paint, Oil paint, water paint, Ink and Pencil.

However, I like to further broaden my horizon by creating digital illustrations and, soon, card invitations. This business is to showcase my creative abilities and how versatile I am, making use of my passion and talent for creativity. Please look around my website to see what I offer and view my creative abilities.
